More Than Just Property Management

Founded on the belief that hosting should be an art form, Orbit Co-Host bridges the gap between property ownership and hospitality excellence.

We noticed a gap in the market: property managers were either too large and impersonal, or too small and inexperienced. Orbit was born to provide the systems of a large corporation with the white-glove touch of a boutique firm.

Our mission is simple: to empower property owners to build wealth through real estate without the headaches of day-to-day operations.
